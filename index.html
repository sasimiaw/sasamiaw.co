Our Story

Welcome to SasaMiaw.CO, your go-to destination for adorable and aesthetic keychains!

Founded in 2023, we started with a simple mission: to spread joy through cute and playful accessories. Our keychains are designed to bring a smile to your face every time you see them.

Our Collections

  • Acrylic Keychains: Durable, colorful, and perfect for everyday use.
  • Plush Keychains: Soft, huggable, and great for bag decorations.
  • Special Editions: Limited designs released seasonally.

Our Values

We believe in quality, creativity, and customer happiness. Each keychain is carefully designed and inspected to ensure it meets our high standards.

What materials are your keychains made of?
We use high-quality materials for all our keychains! Our acrylic keychains are made from durable acrylic with vibrant printing, while our plush keychains are made from soft, premium plush fabric with secure stitching. All materials are safe and non-toxic.

How long does shipping take?
Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for regular shipping and 1-2 business days for express shipping within Indonesia. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships!

Can I request a custom keychain design?
